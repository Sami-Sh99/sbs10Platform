My Platform

  • Modify the Graduate Assistantship Stipends to Fulfil Minimal Needs

    The GA, TA, and RA stipends are insufficient compared to the efforts of graduate students that are fulfilling their duties and do not really help especially during the difficult times and tough situations that our country is going through. I will make every effort to propose a stipend modification that satisfies minimal needs of the students and dollarize the compensations.
    Moreover, most GAs are having transportation issues due to feul limitations and high transport costs. I will demand a transportation allowance that varies based on the student's place of residance to make sure the stipend is left for personal expenses and not only for the road.

  • Strive for More Funding Towards Graduate Student Supports

    I will strive to maintain essential student services such as the financial assistance for those who need support, and advance more funding for research related to thesis and publications.

  • Oppose all tuition increases for students

    I will oppose any tuition increases for both domestic and international students as a result of the dollarization policy and previous tuition increase the university is approaching.

  • Establishing Connections Between Students and the Board

    Historically, AUB graduate students have been disconnected from the issues addressed by the Board of Governors. However, these decisions often have a direct impact on students' academic and career success. As your representative, you would actively promote awareness of the vital role of the board of directors by creating opportunities for students to work with governors. As a result, board decisions can be based on the needs and challenges of graduate students.

  • Quiet Study Spaces

    With the new distancing regulations for COVID safety, Jaffet library no longer preserves a space for graduates. Graduate students in the faculty need a quiet space where they can finish assignments, study for midterms and finals, and work on their thesis without noise. If elected, I will apply for a quality money grant to convert a space in the faculty into a quiet study space for all graduate students to use.
